Submission Guidelines

Deccan Arts Publications.

Guidelines for submitting your story in the 500 Words Fiction Contest

Please read following lines carefully.

  • Eligibility:
    • Any person, with any gender is open to participate in the contest.
    • You must be at least 18 years old at the time of the entry.
    • Both published and unpublished writers are welcome to participate.
    • There are no restrictions based on geographical location.
  • Theme and Genre:
    • Stories can be of any genre (fiction, science fiction, fantasy, mystery, romance, etc.).
    • The theme is open-ended, allowing writers to explore various topics and concepts.
  • Word Limit:
    • Each submission must be a short story and should not exceed 500 words.
    • Each submission must be at least 200 words.
    • Entries exceeding the word limit will be disqualified.
    • Title of the story will be excluded from the word count.
  • Originality:
    • Submissions must be original and previously unpublished.
    • Any AI generated story will be immediately disqualified.
    • Plagiarised work will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Formatting:
    • Submissions must be written in English.
    • Include the title of your story at the top of the first page.
    • Do not include your name or any identifying information on the document itself; submissions are judged anonymously.
  • Submission Process:
    • Submit your story via authorised google form only.
    • Please provide valid Email ID and Mobile phone for qualification.
    • Please also provide brief bio about yourself (50 words max).
    • Please provide a valid mobile address, with UPI payment enabled on the same mobile number.
  • Deadline:
    • The submission deadline is each month's second Monday at 11:59 PM IST.
    • Late submissions will not be considered.
  • Entry Fee:
    • There is no entry fee for this contest.
  • Judging Criteria:
    • Creativity and originality of the story.
    • Clarity of expression and writing style.
    • Adherence to the theme (if specified).
    • Overall impact and emotional resonance of the story.
    • The judges’ view is final and no correspondence will be entered into in connection with the award of the prizes.
  • Prizes:
    • Winner gets Rs 500, Gold* Medal, and certificate of participation.
    • Runners-up gets Silver* Medal, and certificate of participation.
    • Second Runners-up gets bronze* Medal a certificate of participation.
    • Top 10 stories will be included in the upcoming story book collections.
    • Publication of winning stories on our website or in a designated publication.
    • Prize will be distributed over UPI payment mode.
  • Rights and Permissions:
    • By submitting your story, you grant “Deccan Arts” and “Deccan Arts Publication” the right to publish the winning stories on our website or in any related promotional material.
    • You also grant permission to publish your stories as the part of the next 500 word story book.
    • All other rights remain with the author.
  • Notification of Winners:
    • Winners will be notified via email by the 20th day of each month.
    • The results will also be announced on our website and through our social media channels.
  • Disclaimer:
    • “Deccan Arts” and “Deccan Arts Publications” reserves the right to disqualify any entry that does not adhere to the guidelines or for any other reason deemed necessary.

    • We encourage you to craft your stories thoughtfully and creatively.

    Your submission should be a reflection of your unique voice and storytelling ability. Good luck, and we look forward to reading your entries!

    If you have any further queries, please contact us at info.deccanarts@gmail.com
