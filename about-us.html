It All Started with Smoke and Tea.

Around 2 PM, there were 3 friends doing nothing, and suddenly they saw, a small girl. The excitement and happiness are overwhelming to show the new storybook she brought with her father.

What We Saw

New authors and writers often feel discouraged and nervous about their talent. Lack of ideas, low confidence, fear of selling, connection to the publisher. Busy lifestyle and money problems. And they give up their writing skills.

What is Needed

The one platform where new authors and writers can earn from their writing skills. Easy access to the publication opportunity. Monthly motivation to sharpen their writing skills also earn money with the same.

What is Solution

500-word fiction contest, where each month writer will get 5 words and 1 phrase. A writer will write a story in 500 words. The winner gets Rs 500 cash and the top 10 stories will be published as a short story collection.

What is 500 Words Fiction Contest

  • 500 Words Fiction contest is a short story monthly contest where you get a chance to win a Rs 500.00 cash prize, a certificate of participation, and much more than that.
  • Rule:Each month you will get 5 words and 1 phrase, you must write a 200 to 500-word story including the given words and phrases.
  • Winner: gets Rs 500, Gold* Medal, and certificate of participation.
  • Runners-up: gets Silver* Medal, and certificate of participation.
  • Second Runners-up: gets bronze* Medal a certificate of participation.
  • Entry Fee: 0 (Yes, you can enter into contest for free!)
  • Language: English Only
  • Deadline: Jan 29, 2024 23:59 IST
  • Word Limit: 500 words (excluding title and line spaces).
  • AI Entry: Entries must not be generated using AI technology.
  • Multiple Entry: 10 entry per writer is permitted.
  • Age: Entrants must be aged over 18year.
  • Terms: Please read the full 500 words fiction contest complete submission guidelines carefully, before you enter into contest.
