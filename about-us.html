Frequently Asked Questions

Do I retain the rights to my story?

Yes, of course. It's your story. However, we kindly ask all of our winners and runner-ups to allow us to link to their winning stories on our Past Winners page and also into our short story collection book.

Do I need to be a certain age to be eligible to enter?

To be eligible to enter, participants must be at least 18 years old when the battle begins.

Does my story have to be in English?

Your story should be primarily in English since the word limit is set according to the English grammer scale.

Is this all anonymous?

Absolutely. The identities of the authors and judges shall remain anonymous throughout the contest. When the peer judging has wrapped up, writers will be given the option to share their story on their official instagram handle.
