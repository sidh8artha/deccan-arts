Unleash Your Creativity

500-word fiction is Now Open!

Greetings, fellow writers and storytellers!

We are thrilled to announce the launch of the 500-word fiction short story, a celebration of creativity where your words have the power to captivate minds and hearts. If you're passionate about storytelling, this is your chance to shine. Read on to discover the details and how you can be part of this exciting literary adventure.

Learn More Submit Story Register as Writer

Contest Details

Theme : Open

  • We believe in the boundless power of creativity. Therefore, there are no constraints on the theme—let your imagination run wild. Whether it's a tale of fantasy, a slice of life, or a gripping mystery, the stage is yours.

Prompt :

  • 1st prompt : 5 words (Item, beginning, Match, Centuary, & Happiness)
  • 2nd prompt : 1 phrase (Wake Up Call)

Prizes :

  • First Prize: ₹500.00, Gold* Medal, Certificate of participation.
  • Second Prize: Silver* Medal, Certificate of Participation.
  • Third prize: Bronze* Medal, Certificate of Participation.
  • Top 10 Story will published as a Book.

Submission Period : Five Days Only

  • Time is of the essence! The contest is open for submissions for five days, providing an exciting opportunity to showcase your storytelling prowess in a short timeframe.

Word Count : 400-500 Words

  • Craft a concise yet impactful story within the specified word limit. Challenge yourself to convey a powerful message or weave an intriguing plot in this compact space.

Genre : Open to All

  • Whether you're a fan of fantasy, fiction, non-fiction, or poetry, all genres are welcome. This is your chance to experiment and explore the vast landscape of storytelling.

Eligibility : Open for All

  • No matter where you are in the world, this contest is open to writers of all ages and backgrounds. We believe in the universality of storytelling and welcome diverse voices.

Entry Fee : Free

  • In our commitment to fostering creativity, there is no entry fee. We want to ensure that everyone has an equal opportunity to share their stories without any financial barriers.

How to Participate

  • Craft Your Story : Create a captivating short story between 400 and 500 words. Let your creativity shine, and explore the realms of storytelling.
  • Submit Your Entry: Visit our submission page here and follow the simple instructions to submit your masterpiece. Remember, the clock is ticking, so don't miss the submission window!
  • Spread the Word: Share the news with fellow writers and storytellers. The more, the merrier! Let's create a community of storytellers.

Judging Criteria :

  • Entries will be judged based on creativity, originality, and the impact of the story. Our distinguished panel of judges is eager to discover the next great storyteller, and it could be you!

Important Dates :

  • Contest Launch : 15th January 2024, 11:00 AM IST
  • Submission Period : 20th January 2024, 10:00 PM IST
  • Winner Announcement : 25th January 2024, 11:00 AM IST

Submission Guidelines :

  • Write A story with above 5 words and 1 phrase.
  • Word count should be 400 to 500 words.
  • Write it in 5days.
  • Win cash and winning certificate for and chance to publish as book.
  • visit submission-guidelines for more details.

Join the Conversation :

  • Connect with fellow participants and enthusiasts on social media using the hashtag #500wordsficition. Share your writing journey, exchange ideas, and be part of a vibrant community.

Ready, Set, Write!

Now that the stage is set, it's time to unleash your creativity. We can't wait to be transported into the worlds you create and witness the magic of your storytelling.

Best of luck to all participants! May your words resonate and leave an indelible mark on our literary tapestry.

Happy writing!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I retain the rights to my story?

Yes, of course. It's your story. However, we kindly ask all of our winners and runner-ups to allow us to link to their winning stories on our Past Winners page and also into our short story collection book.

Do I need to be a certain age to be eligible to enter?

To be eligible to enter, participants must be at least 18 years old when the battle begins.

Does my story have to be in English?

Your story should be primarily in English since the word limit is set according to the English grammer scale.

Is this all anonymous?

Absolutely. The identities of the authors and judges shall remain anonymous throughout the contest. When the peer judging has wrapped up, writers will be given the option to share their story on their official instagram handle.
